The “I Care” Sticker Hub

Thank you for reading [The Invisible Hand of Humanism] and for wanting to help.

This page gives you everything you need to join the “I Care” initiative with one small, visible gesture.

Each sticker says:
“I care about [VALUE] and I want to help.”

Choose the value that matters most to you, download the sticker, and display it on your social media profiles or in physical spaces such as your laptop, window, office door, bicycle, or notebook.

3. For organisations and NGOs

If you represent an NGO, school, university, company, or association, you are welcome to use these stickers in your own communication. The only condition is that the core design remains unchanged so that we all speak with one shared, recognisable visual signal.

The “I Care” initiative is non-partisan and decentralised. It is not a political party or a campaign, but a civic gesture: a way for citizens to say, quietly but visibly, “I care about our shared future, and I am willing to contribute.”
